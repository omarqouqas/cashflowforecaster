## Daily Outreach Routine

```
Morning (30 min):
1. Search Twitter: "freelance" + "overdraft" OR "broke" OR "bills"
2. Find 5 people complaining about money stress
3. Reply helpfully (not pitching)
4. DM with soft offer: "Built something that might help, want to try it free?"

Evening (15 min):
5. Engage with any replies to your Twitter thread
6. Check Facebook group activity, comment helpfully
7. Track signups in PostHog
```
